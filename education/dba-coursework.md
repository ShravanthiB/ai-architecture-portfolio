## Key Insights from Year 1

1. **AI is powerful but requires careful architecture**: Raw AI capabilities need proper system design to be useful in business.

2. **Integration is critical**: Most AI value comes from integrating with existing systems, not standalone models.

3. **Governance matters early**: Safety, ethics, and governance should be designed in, not bolted on.

4. **Business context is essential**: AI must solve real business problems, not just demonstrate technical capability.

5. **The field is rapidly evolving**: Continuous learning and adaptation are essential.

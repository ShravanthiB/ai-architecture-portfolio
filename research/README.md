## Research Focus

Exploring dissertation topics at the intersection of business value, enterprise architecture, and AGI systems.

## Areas of Interest

### 1. Enterprise AGI Architecture
How should enterprises architect autonomous AI systems for safe, scalable deployment?

**Key Questions**:
- What architectural patterns enable safe AGI deployment?
- How do we govern autonomous agents in business contexts?
- What integration patterns connect AGI with existing systems?
- How do we measure and ensure AGI system safety?

### 2. Multi-Agent Systems in Business
Design patterns and frameworks for multi-agent AI systems in enterprise applications.

**Key Questions**:
- How should agents coordinate in business processes?
- What supervision patterns ensure quality and safety?
- How do we handle agent failures and recovery?
- What organizational structures support multi-agent systems?

### 3. AI Integration Architecture
Patterns and frameworks for integrating AI capabilities with traditional enterprise systems.

**Key Questions**:
- What integration patterns work best for AI systems?
- How do we handle AI-specific concerns (latency, cost, errors)?
- What architectural evolution is needed for AI-native enterprises?
- How do we balance flexibility and governance?

### 4. AI Governance & Safety
Frameworks for responsible deployment of autonomous AI in business.

**Key Questions**:
- What governance models work for autonomous agents?
- How do we architect safety into AI systems?
- What monitoring and observability is required?
- How do we ensure compliance and auditability?
